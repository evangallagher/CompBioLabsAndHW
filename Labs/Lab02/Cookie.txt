Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe and Instructions 
1 cup salted butter 
1 cup white sugar 
1 cup brown sugar 
2 tsp vanilla 
2 eggs 
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking powder 

INSTRUCTONS: 
1. Add melted butter and sugars into mixing bowl. 
2. Begin stirring and mix in eggs, flour, and baking powder  
3. Once even, mix in chocolate chips 
4. Preheat oven to 350
5. Place palm sized dough into oven. 
6. Leave for 15 minutes
